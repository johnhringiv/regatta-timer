Privacy Policy

Regatta Timer for Wear OS

The short version

Regatta Timer collects no data. None.

The complete version

  • The app has no network access — it makes no connections of any kind.
  • It collects no personal information, no usage analytics, no identifiers, no location.
  • It contains no advertising and no third-party SDKs.
  • The only data it stores is the state of a running timer (which start sequence you armed and when the gun fired), kept locally on the watch so a race survives an app restart. It never leaves the device and is cleared when you reset the timer.
  • It requests no permissions beyond vibration (haptic signals) and the wake lock used to keep the countdown running.

Google Play itself reports aggregate install statistics and crash diagnostics to the developer; that platform-level collection is governed by Google's privacy policy and your device's diagnostics settings, not by this app.

Questions: play@johnhringiv.com or GitHub issues.

Effective July 2026. If this policy ever changes, the change will be visible in the public edit history.
